About Mirjana Zidar, UI Designer in Montenegro

My Story

I’m Mirjana Zidar, a UI designer and photographer based in Herceg Novi, Montenegro.

Design has always been on the periphery of my interests, and it wasn’t until very recently that I realized I possessed a passion for design that rivals the excitement I feel for photography.

I began my design journey in October 2019 with the bootcamp via CareerFoundry. Recently finished the course. I'm thrilled to be working as a freelance UI Designer.

I'm passionate about learning new things through design. I love creating things that I could see people interact with and enjoy. Able to bring ideas to the table and deliver them through.

In my free time, you can find me reading books, hiking, swimming or spending time with family or friends.
