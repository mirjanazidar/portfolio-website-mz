Foliage Online Plant Shop

The place to shop unique and rare aroids provided by the Foliage’s green house where rare plants of the highest quality are grown. Foliage is designed to enable users to find rare plants by using search features and detailed filters.

My Process

User Flow

I developed my user flow based on user stories from the brief and what functionality was essential based on those user needs. This process allowed me to understand the goal of every page and to set up the most common way to perform an action.

Wireframes Evolution

By using my user flow I was able to create wireframes to get the basic functionality of the app layed out.

Typography & Colors

The step of choosing colors and typography was equally important as the other steps. I wanted to show the whole atmosphere of having plants indoors and decided to use simple colors so the plant images can dominate the web site.

Shop Page & Filtering Options

The home page offers instant access to the shop but also has featured offers with discounts, favourites and available products. The Foliage is providing the option of filtering by discount, avaliable plants, easy care and pet friendly. You can sort Foliage’s inventory by price, newest and best seling plants.

Product Details

There's a proper way to care of indoor plants. Here you can discover what is the proper care for a wanted plant, is it safe for pets or does it have enough light in your home.

Add to Cart

After viewing a plant profile, users are able to access the inventory and place multiple items in a shopping cart without having to register. It's easy to find the rare plant from your sofa, on the bus or even at your desk. Just add items to your basket and checkout.

Conclusion

For this project, I designed all of the branding and visual identity for an online plant shop. I also created the user interface design, as well as the website and a cart and shopping process
